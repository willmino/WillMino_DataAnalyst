Portfolio Project #2:
US and Global Health Metrics

In this capstone project for the Data Science certicate, US county proximity to power plants and emission levels were used to train machine learning models to predict disease incidences like cancer and respiratory disease. Additionally, global health metrics per country such as GDP per capita, healthcare spending, poverty, etc. were used to train machine learning models to predict a country's life expectancy. Notable ML models: XGBoost, LogisticRegression, RandomForestRegressor.
